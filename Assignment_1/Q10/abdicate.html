Meaning


Abdicate: to renounce or relinquish a throne, right, power, claim, responsibility, or the like, especially in a formal manner.


Uses


  • Use 1: The aging founder of the firm decided to abdicate.
  • Use 2: King Edward VIII of England abdicated the throne in 1936.
